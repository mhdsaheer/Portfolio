Muhammed Saheer

Hi, I’m Muhammed Saheer — a front-end developer and multimedia specialist from Kerala, India. I have a background in multimedia and over the past year, I’ve been combining design and code to build clean, responsive interfaces. I enjoy creating user experiences that are both engaging and easy to use.
